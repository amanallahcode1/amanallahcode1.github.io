About Me

A creative problem-sovler

Since the young age of 13, I've always known I wanted to become a software engineer. I carefully planned for my path to my goal by getting a degree in CS. However, I realized that I spent too much time on academics and theory.

There was too much theory in college and a ton of dull tasks. I wanted to have an impact by tinkering and solving unique problems. Joining App Academy after completing my bachelors at UCLA was one of the best decisions of my life.

I not only got the practical experience I longed for, but I also had the chance to work in a team setting almost everyday, which honed my ability to collaborate. Outside of coding, I enjoy playing soccer, reading about cosmology, and listening to hip-hop.
